Why Bungie’s sci-fi game will never die.​

The game keeps growing​

  • New Season: Every few months, new stories, missions and gear are added.
  • Big Updates: Expansions like the witch queen, lightfall and the final shape bring big changes.​
  • Player Feedback: Bungie listens to players and makes improvements.​
  • Living World: The game’s story changes based on the in-game events.​

The Community

The players are keeping the game alive​

  • Content Creators: Streamers and Youtubers upload content daily.
  • Clans: Players team up and make friends.​
  • Fans: Players keep coming back to play the game.​
  • The game is fun: Raids, events, missions, dungeons and the crucible keep people engaged.​

The Gameplay is so fun​

It's easy for beginners to play the game​, but it's tough to master the game​​

  • Great Combat: Destiny 2 is known for the shooting because of the variety of different styles of weapons.​
  • Tough Challenge: Raids, Nightfalls and Trials are for skilled players only.​
  • Fans: Players keep coming back to play the game.​
  • Play Anywhere: Crossplay lets friends play together on any platform. Destiny 2 is coming to mobile soon.​

Destiny 2 will last forever​​

Why is it not going anywhere​​​

  • Bungie always updates the game every Tuesday to keep destiny 2 fresh.
  • The world has endless stories to tell.​
  • The community will never stop supporting the game and bungie.​
